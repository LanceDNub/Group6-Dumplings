WHAT IS WASTE DISPOSAL?

According to Britannica.com

Waste disposal is, the collection, processing, and recycling or deposition of the waste materials of human society. Waste is classified by source and composition. Broadly speaking, waste materials are either liquid or solid in form, and their components may be either hazardous or inert in their effects on health and the environment. The term waste is typically applied to solid waste, sewage (wastewater), hazardous waste, and electronic waste.

How does the workshop help you and the environment?

This workshop will really help our environment in many ways. It will not just benefit our environment it will also benefit everyone. You'll get a lot of knowledge and skills on how to take care of our environment and new ways on how to properly dispose of waste. By taking care of our environment, you can make new products by disposing waste in a way of recycling, reusing, reducing. Example, you can make a pot out of bottles, trash cans and more! This workshop will also help us protect our environment. You will learn some awesome tips on how we can protect our environment and how to make waste disposal more fun and easy. You will also experience different activities that will further help you in developing your skills.

Additional Information about the Workshop:

In this workshop we have 3 sessions each consisting of 3 hours per session. In each session we have many different variations of activities we can do during a session. The first session consists of breaking the ice or getting to know the topic and the workshop itself. The second session will be all about preparing for the major activities that are prepared for you. The third session is all about the execution of your outputs together with making your final outputs and of course the presentation. For questions we have an open forum allotted for you questions, and before we end a session we will always have an evaluation and entertainment before we finish.
